🕷️ THE MELANCHOLY OF SAMMY SWELL 🕸️

⌿ NOTE I ⍀ This is a video game project not a book nor a blog. This is an early vision of the project, everything is subject to decay.

⌿ NOTE II ⍀ The game's main attraction is its story. Gameplay is minimal (mostly exploration), most of the game is text-based conversations between two characters or one character and their inner thoughts.

2018 OCTOBER

Sammy has just enrolled in the University of Game Development with a goal to create a game that would positively impact players. But he couldn't finish creating any game, as he was lacking in his artistic style. Determined to find a friend who excelled in drawing, he went to the Game Design department.

Despite his efforts, he couldn't find any students interested in drawing in the style he envisioned. Most of them were focused on realistic 3D and rounded-edge drawings. So he gave up and planned to just hire an online artist. So he walked to his car to head home, and there she was sitting in the front seat of his car, waiting for him —

his childhood friend M

as she was holding a tablet with both of her hands and her eyes full of excitement said "Hey Sammy I have something to show you" and she flipped her tablet and showed him
S: "Sorry, didn't watch the movie but her name is Kiki, right?"
M: "Yes yes ... So what do you think?"
S: (took him a second) "Wait, you drew that?! It's amazing, I love it. Since when can you draw?"
M: "I watched a few videos online... do you really like it?"
S: "Yes yes it's really nice ... I'm just surprised."
M: "I know I still have a long way to go, it's just that I wanted to help you with your game."
S: "M — you don't have to do this I can—"
M: "Stop, you helped me a lot and this is the least I can do. But on one condition — I want to add one thing to your game."
 and so they set out to make a video game together

2019 OCTOBER

After a few failed projects, Sammy sold his first game, a horror game about a boy stuck in a hospital elevator. Unfortunately, the game flopped, with only 27 copies sold. Nonetheless, that didn't bother him as much, but he felt bad for M. She had worked so hard with him on the game, and he didn't earn much to pay her for her diligence. Also, her birthday was coming up in November, so he wanted to give her a special gift to show his gratitude.

So he thought about how she enjoys playing co-op games with him and how she loves the scenery and the music more than the gameplay itself. So he decided to create a single-level video game just for the two of them.

⌿ NOTE III ⍀ The game is designed for two players, in which the screen is split in half just like the pictures below. There is a window between them and they have to walk to the window to be able to talk to each other and progress.

He used some AI to make the characters for him and put together some night sky and a garden place. Just a small place for the two of them to sit, to let her know how much she meant to him. And the game was named

half princess

It was the best day of his life. They kept playing for three days straight, talking about everything that came to their minds. They kept adding things to the level, as one of them would suggest something and the other would agree and work on it. With that, they created six levels for both of them to enjoy. Until...

2022 OCTOBER

Poor Sammy has spent all these days trying to find someone to fill the space she left in his game. He tried playing with friends and strangers, but he couldn't stop thinking of her and the fact that he wouldn't see her again. He needed her more than anything in the world, so he got the idea of making an AI and teaching it how to be like her so he could get back that feeling of playing with her. And then there was Pleione.
