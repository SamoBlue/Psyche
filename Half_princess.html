The Melancholy of Sammy Swell



Note I This is an early vision of the Story, and everything is subject to change.While it may seem like the narrative is progressing quickly, please note that this only 10% of the whole story. I might fill in the gaps if the readers like it


The year is 2016, and Sammy has just enrolled in the University of Game Development with a goal to create a game that would positively impact players. he coudn't finish creating any game as he was lacking in his artistic style. Determined to find a friend who excelled in character drawing, he went to the Game Design department. Despite his efforts, he couldn't find any students interested in drawing in the style he envisioned. Most of them were focused on 3D realistic and rounded-edge drawings. So he gave up and was planning to go home and just hire an artist online. But as he was walking towards the bus station, he saw a girl sitting on the grass next to a tree, holding a tablet. As he glanced at what she was drawing, it was...

Note II The game's main attraction is its story. Gameplay is minimal (more like exploration), most of the game is just text-based conversations between two characters or one character and his/her inner thoughts.



Note III The game is designed for two players, in which the screen is split vertically just like the pictures there is a window between them and they have to walk to the windows to be able to talk to each other and solve the puzzles togther.
