The Melancholy of Sami Swell



I This is an early vision of the game, everything is subject to change. The story draws inspiration from real-life stories mostly my story.

II The game's main attraction is its story. Gameplay is minimal (more like exploration), most of the game is just text-based conversations between two characters or one character and his/her inner thoughts.

III The game is designed for two players, in which the screen is split vertically just like the pictures there is a window between them and they have to walk to the windows to be able to talk to each other and solve the puzzles togther.
