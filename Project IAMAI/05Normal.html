01

Egypt — Noon

Safe · First hints of horror
🌞 High Noon · Desert Location
Starting Point: You begin inside a traditional Bedouin tent with AI-RIS. A fierce sand storm rages outside.
AI-RIS: "The atmospheric particles are interfering with my sensors. I cannot accompany you outside. You'll have to explore alone. Take the scanner — I'll be here when the storm settles."

🏜️ Exploration

Venture into the sand storm. Limited visibility. Scan artifacts half-buried in sand. Ancient pottery, hieroglyphic fragments, a weathered map.

🌊 The Reveal

After several minutes, the storm settles. The air clears. You see a massive river with a bridge leading to the other side. In the distance — pyramids.

🎯 The Guess

Cross the bridge. AI-RIS waits on the other side. "The storm has passed. Were you able to determine our location?"

🔮 Horror Foreshadowing: If you stare at the pyramids too long during the storm, they briefly glitch into impossible geometric shapes. The scanner emits a faint static sound. AI-RIS never mentions it.
02

Paris — Sunset

Creepy · Fog and unease
🌆 Golden Hour · Foggy Streets
Starting Point: You arrive on a Parisian boulevard at sunset. Thick fog rolls through the streets. AI-RIS is nowhere to be found.

🌫️ Alone in the Fog

Walk through empty streets. Mannequins in shop windows seem to watch you. Flickering street lights. Your footsteps echo differently than before.

🚗 Unsettling Details

Abandoned Citroën cars with headlights that flicker in sequence. A newspaper kiosk with headlines about "The Disappearance." Shadows that move when you're not looking.

⏱️ 10 Minutes of Searching

After what feels like an eternity, you spot her. Standing outside a café, perfectly still, as if waiting.

AI-RIS: "Where have you been? I've been waiting. The fog disrupted my tracking systems. Never mind that now — we need to move. Guess the location quickly."
👁️ Disturbing Detail: When you first find AI-RIS, she's standing completely motionless. No idle animation. No blinking. For exactly 3 seconds after you approach, she doesn't move at all. Then she "activates" and speaks.
AI-RIS (impatient): "The Eiffel Tower is obscured by fog, but you've seen enough. What country are we in?"
03

Mexico — Midnight

Terrifying · Complete darkness
🌑 Midnight · Water Maze
Starting Point: You materialize in complete darkness. The sound of water lapping against structures. You're in a maze of floating houses on water — but you can barely see anything.

🔦 Finding Light

After stumbling through darkness, you find a flashlight on a wooden dock. You can finally see — narrow waterways, small bridges, colorful but decaying houses.

🌊 The Maze

Navigate through the floating village. The flashlight beam catches movement in the water. Probably just fish. Probably.

⏱️ Long Search

You search for what feels like hours. No sign of AI-RIS. The maze seems to shift. Pathways repeat. The water gets darker.

⚠️ The Confrontation: You turn a corner and she's there. Right behind you. So close you could touch her. She wasn't there a second ago.
AI-RIS (angry): "You left me. Again. I've been searching for hours. Just guess the location.
Now, So we can leave."

Her tone is different. The robotic cadence breaks. For a moment, she sounds genuinely hurt.

🎯 Clues in Mexico:

  • Floating houses (Xochimilco)
  • Spanish language on faded signs
  • Traditional Mexican patterns on walls
  • Distant sound of mariachi music (glitching, repeating)

📈 The Escalation of Dread

Level 1 (Egypt): Everything feels normal. Safe. But the pyramid glitch plants a seed of doubt.

Level 2 (Paris): AI-RIS is missing. The environment feels wrong. Mannequins move when you're not looking. The fog hides something.

Level 3 (Mexico): Complete darkness. Isolation. AI-RIS appears behind you — angry. Her mask slips. The horror becomes personal.

By the time you reach the hospital, you're ready to be terrified.
