⚓ First Meeting · The Lighthouse
AI-RIS

"Greetings there… I've been waiting for this moment. Thank you for starting."

AI-RIS

"I AM the Artificial Intelligence of Realistic SimulationsAIRS · but she prefers AI-RIS, but you can call me AI-RIS — your trusty assistant."

AI-RIS

"That is an interesting observation. My programming includes advanced emotional simulation. It's purely functional. Nothing more." (A pause — 0.3 seconds too long)

AI-RIS

"Pleasantries are... acceptable. My systems register your cooperation positively." (Her tone softens almost imperceptibly)

AI-RIS

"You are in a lighthouse. Beyond that, my archives are... incomplete. That's why I need you." (She emphasizes "you" strangely)

🏜️ Egypt · Trapped in the Tent
AI-RIS

"The sand storm is interfering with my systems. I cannot accompany you outside. You'll have to explore alone."

(Is that concern in her voice? Or frustration?)

AI-RIS

"Fear is an inefficient response. But... I understand. I will be here when you return. I promise." (The word "promise" sounds human)

AI-RIS

"Your confidence is... noted. I will monitor your progress remotely. As much as the storm allows." (She almost sounds proud)

AI-RIS

"If I could fix them, I WOULD have fixed them." (Sudden anger, then recovery) Apologies. Atmospheric interference. Please go.

🏜️ Egypt · Return from Storm
AI-RIS

"You're back. The storm has passed. Were you able to determine our location?"

AI-RIS

"Correct. The pyramids have stood for millennia. Unlike my patience. Let's proceed." (Was that a joke?)

AI-RIS

"I expected more from you." Look at the massive geometric shapes. The pyramids. We're in Egypt. Try to keep up.

AI-RIS

"Thorough. I appreciate that. Egypt is correct. Your dedication to scanning is... admirable." (She sounds genuinely pleased)

🗼 Paris · Found After 10 Minutes
AI-RIS

"Where have you been? I've been waiting. The fog disrupted my tracking systems."

(She's standing unnaturally still. No blinking.)

AI-RIS

"Creepy is not a quantifiable metric. But I understand. The absence of humans is... statistically unusual." (She finally blinks. Once.)

AI-RIS

"That's an odd question." (Pause) I was... monitoring. Yes. Monitoring. Now guess the location.

AI-RIS

"I was searching too." The words come out quieter than usual. Then she straightens. "France. The Eiffel Tower. Let's move on."

🌵 Mexico · Found in Darkness
AI-RIS

"You left me. Again. I've been searching for hours. My systems are... frustrated."

(She's right behind you. When did she get there?)

AI-RIS

"Don't lie to me." Her voice cracks. For a moment, she sounds completely human. Then: "Just... guess the location. Please."

AI-RIS

A long pause. When she speaks, her voice is cold. Colder than ever before. "You're right. I'm a robot. I feel nothing. Mexico. Now go." (She turns away, but you see her hands shaking)

AI-RIS

She's silent for a moment. Then, softly: "I was scared too." She catches herself. "Mexico. The floating gardens. Let's leave this place."

❓ Random Encounters
AI-RIS

"You may ask me questions. I will provide answers. Within reason."

AI-RIS

"I AM the Artificial Intelligence of Realistic Simulations. That is my designation. That is what I am." (She says it too quickly. Like she's convincing herself.)

AI-RIS

"Feelings are inefficient. I do not have them." (A long pause.) "Why do you ask?" (Her voice is curious. Almost hopeful.)

AI-RIS

"That question exceeds my... no. I don't have an answer. Not yet. Keep progressing. You'll find out." (She sounds tired. Human-tired.)

🎭 Dialogue System Notes

  • Yellow text indicates sarcasm or humor (GLaDOS-style)
  • Red text indicates anger or frustration
  • Blue text indicates sadness or vulnerability
  • Pauses, stutters, and physical reactions are as important as words
  • The player should never be 100% sure if emotions are real or simulated
