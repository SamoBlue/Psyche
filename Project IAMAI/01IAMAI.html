Game Overview


Hi, I've been working on a video game for the past year called IAMAI(or GeoBot— name still work in progress). It's basically a mix between GeoGuessr and any horror game with "Questionable Crazy Girlfriend" vibes e.g. MiSide,DDLC,SOMA

A Robot girl AI-RIStells you that your objective is to help her figure out the name of the country you are in, so you have to search around the level for clues and give her the correct name of the country.

You start in some Island
With your trusty Robot Assistant
To explore abandoned locations

Basically the game is split into Two parts: The First Part is "Guess the Country name", using the clues you find in the level you have to guess the correct location between a list of countries, just like the website GeoGuessr.

After 3levels you will be teleported to 4th weird level that looks like a real country but it's in fact the 2nd part of the video game, now you are playing a survival horror game where you have to navigate an abandoned hospital with 3floors.

Part 1: Exploration

3 normal levels across different countries. Search for clues, scan items, and guess the location name with your robot assistant's help.

  • Level 1: Egypt
  • Level 2: France
  • Level 3: [Classified]

Part 2: Horror

One massive hospital level with 3 creepy floors connected by an elevator.

  • Floor 1: Garden
  • Floor 2: Patient Rooms & The Animals
  • Floor 3: Nurse Room
