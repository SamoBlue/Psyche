IAMAI

A mysterious exploration where Geogeusser meets the Rusty Lake.

Game Overview


Hi, I’ve been working on a video game for the past year called IAMAI (or GeoBot — name still work in progress). It’s basically a mix between GeoGuessr and any horror game with “Crazy Girlfriend” vibes.

A Robot girl tells you that your objective is to help her figure out the name of the country you are in, so you have to search around the level for clues and give her the right name of the country.

After 4 locations you arive at an abandoned hospital and the robot girl is nowhere to be seen and the horror part of the game starts, where you have to solve diffrent kind of puzzles and try to find the girl and escape.

You start in some Island
With your trusty Robot Assisstant
To explore abandoned locations

Genre

  • Exploration.
  • Puzzle.
  • Psychological horror.
  • Multiple Endings.

Core Mechanics

  • Scan glitchy Object to reveal them.
  • Guess real-world locations from environmental clues.
  • Interact with AI-RIS for hints.
  • Discover hidden horror twists.

Unique Selling Points

  • Map exploration meets subtle horror.
  • Authentic landmarks with surreal deviations.
  • Atmospheric horror.
  • Really mysterious Robot companion
