PART 1: EXPLORATION + GUESS THE COUNTRY

First-Person Interactive Exploration

Each level has the same gameplay loop: First you need to find AIRIS so she can give you a brief introduction about the level (Country) you are currently in. After that she tells you to come back to her when you are ready to guess the country name. At this point you are free to explore the level as much as you want or try to guess the level right away. Of course you can't brute force your way by guessing every country — you only have one try. If the player chooses to ignore AIRIS and scan the level and come back to her, she will still have her interaction dialogue play at first, then she tells you to guess after.

🎮 Core Loop

  • Find AIRIS for level introduction
  • Explore for environmental clues
  • Scan items for confirmation
  • Return to AIRIS to guess country name
  • Unlock next level

    • 🌍 Environmental Interaction

    Read signs, examine objects, discover atmosphere, buy drinks, watch TV and explore side areas

    Many of the interactive elements are purely for immersion — with no story impact.

    ⏱️ Player Freedom

    Stay in each map as long as you want. Guess correctly in the first minute, or explore every corner for hours.
    The choice is yours.

    📝 Example Gameplay Flow:

    As you enter the level you see English Streets names and tall buildings in the distance → Not 100% sure → You scan items around you → Find a Happy Meal box and cars with american plate number → Confidently guess "USA" → AIRIS responds and unlocks next location.

    1. Talk to AIRIS to tell you the level objective
    2. Explore the level for weird artifacts
    3. Scan the artifact and try to guess its name
    4. Scan them all or try guess the location
    5. Go back to AIRIS to tell her your guess
    6. Find the exit and go to the next location
    PART 2: HORROR SURVIVAL

    Find AIRIS in the Abandoned Hospital

    The scanner is gone. Now you have an inventory. Your only goal: find AIRIS, who has gone missing somewhere in this hospital.

    🏥 Hospital Structure

    Single massive level with 3 floors connected by elevator. You have to go back and forth between floors to help the animals.

    🐄 The Animal Sequence

    Find the 3 animals on the 2nd Floor. They each request specific items. Bring them what they want, and they'll give you items to help find AIRIS.

    📦 Inventory System

    Pick up items, carry them, and use them where needed. The interact button now picks up objects instead of scanning.

    🚨 Chase Sequence Status

    Not yet implemented, but the game files already support running. Chase sequences may be added later depending on testing feedback.

    Gameplay Comparison

    🔆 Part 1: Exploration

    • Primary tool: Scanner
    • Objective: Guess country name
    • Interaction: Scan Artifacts
    • Freedom: Complete - stay as long as you want
    • Pacing: Relaxed, curiosity-driven

    🌑 Part 2: Horror

    • Primary tool: Inventory
    • Objective: Find AIRIS or escape
    • Interaction: Pick up and use items
    • Freedom: Linear sequence required
    • Pacing: Tense, survival-focused

    🧪 Gameplay Testing Questions

    These are the key questions that need answers through player testing:

    Q1: Will players be interested in just walking around, scanning items, and trying to guess the country they're in?

    Q2: Do players want more countries to guess? Harder ones?

    Q3: Are players more interested in the story or the horror part?

    Q4: Do players want more interactivity with the world, or more text from AIRIS?

    Q5: Should chase sequences be added to the hospital level?

    These options represent possible player feedback.
