Normal Levels

01

Egypt — Noon

Safe · First hints of horror
🌞 High Noon · Desert Location
Inside the tent · Sandstorm rages
After the storm · Pyramids revealed
Starting Point: You begin inside a traditional Bedouin tent. A fierce sandstorm rages outside. AIRIS cannot accompany you — her systems would be damaged.

🏜️ Exploration

Venture into the sandstorm. Limited visibility forces you to rely on the scanner. Find artifacts half-buried in sand — ancient pottery, hieroglyphic fragments, a weathered map.

🌊 The Reveal

After several minutes, the storm settles. The air clears. A massive river stretches before you with a bridge leading to the other side. In the distance — the pyramids.

🎯 The Guess

Cross the bridge. AIRIS waits on the other side. The clues point to Egypt — the Nile, the pyramids, the desert landscape. One guess. Make it count.

🔮 Horror Foreshadowing: If you stare at the pyramids too long during the storm, they briefly glitch into impossible geometric shapes. The scanner emits static. AIRIS never mentions it.
02

Paris — Sunset

Creepy · Fog and unease
🌆 Golden Hour · Foggy Streets
Foggy boulevard · Empty streets
-------------
Starting Point: You arrive on a Parisian boulevard at sunset. Thick fog rolls through the streets. AIRIS is nowhere to be found.

🌫️ Alone in the Fog

Walk through empty streets. Mannequins in shop windows seem to watch you. Flickering street lights. Your footsteps echo differently — metallic, hollow.

🚗 Unsettling Details

Abandoned Citroën cars with headlights that flicker in sequence. A newspaper kiosk displays headlines about "The Disappearance." Shadows move when you're not looking.

⏱️ 10 Minutes of Searching

After what feels like an eternity, you spot her. Standing outside a café, perfectly still, as if waiting. She doesn't acknowledge you until you're close.

👁️ Disturbing Detail: When you first find AIRIS, she's completely motionless. No idle animation. No blinking. For exactly 3 seconds after you approach, she doesn't move at all. Then she "activates."

🎯 Clues in Paris:

  • Eiffel Tower visible through fog
  • French language on signs
  • Wide boulevards (Haussmann architecture)
  • Baguettes in abandoned bakeries

Mexico — Midnight ------------- WIP

WIP
🌑 Midnight · Water Maze
Floating houses · Complete darkness
Flashlight beam · Movement in water
Starting Point: You materialize in complete darkness. The sound of water lapping against structures. You're in a maze of floating houses — but you can barely see anything.

🔦 Finding Light

After stumbling through darkness, you find a flashlight on a wooden dock. Now you can see — narrow waterways, small bridges, colorful but decaying houses.

🌊 The Maze

Navigate through the floating village. The flashlight beam catches movement in the water. Probably just fish. Probably. The pathways seem to shift when you're not looking.

⏱️ Long Search

You search for what feels like hours. No sign of AIRIS. The maze repeats. Pathways loop. The water gets darker. Then — she's behind you.

⚠️ The Confrontation: You turn a corner and she's there. Right behind you. So close you could touch her. She wasn't there a second ago. Her voice cracks with genuine anger — and hurt.

🎯 Clues in Mexico:

  • Floating gardens (Xochimilco)
  • Spanish language on faded signs
  • Traditional Mexican patterns on walls
  • Distant sound of mariachi music (glitching, repeating)
